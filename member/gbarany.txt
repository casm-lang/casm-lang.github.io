== About ==
Gergö Barany is a postdoctoral researcher at Inria Paris, in the Gallium research team. 
He works on the CompCert verified compiler and compiler optimizations in general.
Previously he was a postdoc at CEA LIST, 
working on information flow analysis in the Frama-C source-code analysis framework.
He holds a PhD at the Institute of Computer Languages at Vienna University of Technology, 
where he was a research assistant.
