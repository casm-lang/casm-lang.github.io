== About ==

Philipp Paulweber is a PhD student, teaching and research assistent at the University of Vienna,
holding a Master of Science (MSc, in German Dipl-Ing.) degree in computer engineering from the Vienna University of
Technology since 2014.
</col>

== Practical Experience ==

He was formerly employed at the real-time communication and safety-critical
software/hardware engineering company Time-Triggered Technology located in Vienna
and was involved in automotive software system architecture design and development
applying standards like the ISO 26262 and AUTOSAR.
